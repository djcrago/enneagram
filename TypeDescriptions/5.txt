Type Five: The Investigator

The intense, cerebral type.
Fives are alert, insightful, and curious.
They are able to concentrate and focus on developing complex ideas and skills.
Independent and innovative, they can become preoccupied with their thoughts and imaginary constructs.
They become detached, yet high-strung and intense.
They typically have problems with isolation, eccentricity, and nihilism.
At their best, healthy Fives are visionary pioneers, often ahead of their time and able to see the world in an entirely new way.
