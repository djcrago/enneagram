Type Eight: The Challenger

The powerful, dominating type.
Eights are self-confident, strong, and assertive.
Protective, resourceful, and decisive, they can also be proud and domineering.
Eights feel that they must control their environment, often becoming confrontational and intimidating.
They typically have problems with allowing themselves to be close to others.
At their best, healthy Eights are self-mastering---they use their strength to improve others' lives, becoming heroic, magnanimous, and sometimes historically great.
